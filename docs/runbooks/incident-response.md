# Incident Response Runbook

## Severity Levels

### SEV1 - Service Down
- **Criteria:** Complete service outage, revenue impact
- **Response:** Page on-call immediately
- **SLA:** 15 min acknowledgment, 1 hour resolution

### SEV2 - Degraded Service
- **Criteria:** Partial outage, workaround available
- **Response:** Slack alert, monitor closely
- **SLA:** 30 min acknowledgment, 4 hour resolution

### SEV3 - Minor Issue
- **Criteria:** Non-critical bug, no user impact
- **Response:** Ticket for next sprint
- **SLA:** Next business day

## Response Checklist

1. Acknowledge incident
2. Assess severity
3. Form response team
4. Communicate status
5. Resolve and verify
6. Write post-mortem
